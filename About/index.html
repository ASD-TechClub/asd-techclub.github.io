History

We started this club in 2016 as an attempt to provide refuge for Makers.

I started Making from the start of 9th grade when I got my first microcontroller kit, and it’s a wonderful experience. To be able to write a few lines of code and instantly cause an LED to flicker, speaker to buzz, or a motor to whirr.

In my years of Making, I’ve come to find the word synonymous with Hacking. Over time, people have feared the term, but Hacking is nothing more than finding alternative uses of different forms of technology to achieve something remarkable.
